Black Ops One - James Grieshaber, Eben Sorkin
Black Ops One is a low contrast, semi
geometric typeface inspired by military
stencil lettering. It is heavy, sturdy,
punchy, and looks best when used at medium
to large sizes because of the small cuts
found in stencils.
Noto Sans NKo Unjoined - Solomana Kante
Noto is a global font collection for
writing in all modern and ancient languages.
Noto Sans N’Ko Unjoined is an unmodulated
(“sans serif”) design for display texts in
the African N’Ko script. It has 184 glyphs.
Pixelify Sans - Stefie Justprince
Pixelify Sans fonts are a unique style
of typography that originated in the 1980s
with the rise of computer graphics and video
games. Pixel fonts are characterized by
their blocky, pixelated appearance, which
is achieved by using a grid of small, square
pixels to create each letterform.
